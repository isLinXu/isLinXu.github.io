# Accomplishments

This page collects representative outcomes across research, engineering, open-source contribution, and public knowledge curation.

## Research and Evaluation Highlights

- MME related work: co-author level participation in benchmark and multimodal evaluation work, forming an important part of the public research identity
- YOLO-Master direction: representative detection research narrative tied to specialized transformer and MOE style acceleration for real-time detection
- Large-model evaluation practice: public work repeatedly connects MLLM, LLM, benchmark construction, evaluation workflows, and deployment quality
- Resume-level metrics already reflected across the profile include multimodal evaluation gain, large-scale image-text data construction, and practical business recall optimization

## Open-Source and Engineering Highlights

- [LlamaFactory](https://github.com/isLinXu/LlamaFactory): representative contribution direction in efficient fine-tuning, LLM, and VLM engineering
- [paper-list](https://github.com/isLinXu/paper-list): one of the strongest public repositories in the profile, combining paper collection, automation, and open publishing workflow
- [crablet](https://github.com/isLinXu/crablet): practical AI assistant and agentic system building
- [YOLOv8_Efficient](https://github.com/isLinXu/YOLOv8_Efficient): long-standing practical computer vision engineering repository with strong public visibility
- Agent-oriented repositories such as [darwin-agent](https://github.com/isLinXu/darwin-agent), [eval-skills](https://github.com/isLinXu/eval-skills), and related execution or coding-agent work show a clear current shift toward agent systems

## Why These Highlights Matter

Taken together, these highlights show a profile that is not limited to a single paper, repository, or benchmark. A recurring signal is the ability to connect:

- research with evaluation
- evaluation with deployment
- deployment with reusable engineering
- engineering with public documentation and open curation

That combination is one of the clearest patterns in the portfolio.
